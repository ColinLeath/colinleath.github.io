Seeding Justice: cultivating a food secure future in San Diego: An all day conference/festival event to be held on Saturday, March 3rd (first Sat). We envision a day filled with defining, discovering, and doing; strategizing, inspriring, and celebrating. We'll have skill-based workshops (composting, gardening, cooking, etc.), discussions (examining food security in San Diego, globalization, land access, health issues, etc.) and trainings (how do you organize a food focused project, reclaiming space, starting a school garden, etc.) as well as music, kids activities, speakers, garden tours, and of course food!
