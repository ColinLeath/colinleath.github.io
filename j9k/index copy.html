Welcome

I continue to think of Ethan Hughes and the Possibility Alliance—they live without using cars, computers, or electricity. For now, I am using two of those.

Here I publish what I write, draw, and record.

My muse has got me to buy a one-way ticket to Amsterdam on July 1. My plan is to head to Berlin by 7/12 to participate in the ecotopia biketour. One larger goal is to remain abroad until I learn how to support myself without returning to the US to raise funds. Another goal is to live in a land that retains more artefacts from the time before cars. I may make it to Greece, Turkey, and Israel for the winter.

I want to make art others may pay to support or access.

One needs to address others' deepest needs and desires in order to sucessfully sell them something.

What are your needs and desires?
  • Do you want to feel healthy?
  • Do you wish to spend time with people in interesting ways in beautiful places?
  • Do you desire to live in a land where few or no people use cars?
  • Do you wish to balance peace of mind with creative engagement?

In January 2013 I ended the outdoor education work I had been doing. I began to seek a way of living that fit better with my personality. Previous to spending $719 on that plane ticket, my plan was to live out the year in the Goleta / Santa Barbara area without working for money and to watch what happened as I did that. Events conspired to get me to choose to get myself to Europe in time for the bike tour rather than to participate in a training. Buying the ticket left me with about $600. Since then, I've made $1016 by working for temp companies and by cleaning apartments.

The carbon offset for the flight is less than $20. I haven't bought it yet. Years ago Ethan and his partner made a trip to Europe to visit communities there. They sailed, I believe. And there is a big old “one less plane” logo on the bike tour web site. I decided, in spite of all that, to plan to fly. I see where I may be able to take a cruise back to the US in the fall or the spring for $500—or less if I share my cabin—but I saw no easy way and did not have the faith or the desire to try a non-flying solution. I didn't wish to stay still any longer. I'd known about the bike ride for years and had wanted to participate for as long. One of my teachers was a grateful user of airplanes. I don't wish to apologize but to describe my reservations.

In the interest of transparency, I'll document incomes and outgoes here. At least for the time being.

Well, I wrote it all up, but it seems to be for me in the category of secrets to attempt to sell. Let me explain how I imagine that working.
