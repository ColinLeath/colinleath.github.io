Bio:

 

Since 2004, I've facilitated teambuilding programs. Since 2005, I've developed and led programs for groups of up to 100 participants, including the 2006 Junior Leaders Camp for the Kroc Center. In my facilitation, I focus on transcending personal perspectives and on decision-making based on awareness of role in team, organization, and world. Recent influences include John P. Milton, Holacracy, Barry Oshry, Integral Leadership, and Theory U.
